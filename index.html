Description

Beautiful and spacious apartment in the heart of Riga. The apartment is located in a historic building in the beautiful Agenskalns district. The apartment is located on the first floor and has a separate entrance. The apartment has been completely renovated a decade ago and needs some refreshing.

The apartment has a large living room, bedroom, office, bathroom, shower, two toilets, and a hallway. The apartment has a beautiful 1000sqm common garden.

The apartment has gas heating with heated floors, double glazing, fireplace, bath and a shower.

The apartment is located in a quiet area and is close to public transport (Bus to Central Station, Trolley bus to Oldtown and a tram that can take you across Riga is few minutes walk). There is a park with kids playground across the street and Lidl is just a few minutes away.

Located in a beautiful and historic part of Riga. The apartment is perfect for a family that wants more room and a space to go outside. The apartment is vacated and ready for immediate occupancy. Please contact us for more information.
